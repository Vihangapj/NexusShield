Our Story

Born from the
Intelligence World

NexusShield was founded in 2018 by a team of Sri Lankan cyber defenders from Colombo, Kandy, and Galle who saw a gap: enterprises had digital risk, but not enough local security expertise.

We bridged that gap — bringing intelligence-grade defense to the private sector. Today, NexusShield protects 3,400+ organizations across 47 countries, processing 4 trillion threat signals daily.
